    Rover Facts

    Ingenuity is the first aircraft to fly on another planet.(carried by Perseverance)

    Perseverance landed using a sky crane - a rocket-powered descent system.

    Perseverance uses AI to autonomously navigate the Martian surface.

    Rovers use laser-induced breakdown spectroscopy (LIBS) to study rock chemistry.

    The parachute of Perseverance had a secret binary code message.
